Tech Stack

  • Frontend: Bootstrap
  • Styling: Tailwind CSS (optional for modern UI)
  • Backend: Node.js + Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB (Atlas) with Mongoose
  • Authentication: JWT + bcrypt
  • Real-time Features: Socket.IO
  • Deployment: Render

About FeedUp

FeedUp is a community-driven platform designed to let people share their thoughts and ideas anonymously. Every post can be upvoted once, ensuring that the most valuable contributions rise to the top. Whether you want to express yourself freely, discover trending opinions, or explore what others are saying, FeedUp provides a simple and secure space to connect without revealing your identity. Join today to start posting, upvoting, and exploring the FeedUp community.
